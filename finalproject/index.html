Piggy Game

Welcome to the Piggy Game! It's a two-player challenge where victory goes to the one with the most coins in their piggy bank. Aim for high-value coins, and if luck is on your side, keep flipping until you're satisfied with your haul before passing the turn. Beware of Fake Coins because they are illegal, and if you draw one, you lose all your money. And if you land on a $1 coin, your luck runs out, and it's the other player's turn. Ready to play?
