Piggy Game

Welcome to the Piggy Game!

It's a two-player challenge where the goal is to collect the most coins in your piggy bank. Here's how to play:

  1. Each turn, you'll have the chance to flip a coin.
  2. Aim for high-value coins to maximize your winnings.
  3. You can keep flipping coins until you decide to stop and pass the turn to your opponent.
  4. But beware! There are Fake Coins in the mix. If you draw one, you lose all your money.
  5. Additionally, if you land on a $1 coin, your turn ends, and it's your opponent's chance to play.

Ready to begin? Let's see who can fill their piggy bank with the most coins!
